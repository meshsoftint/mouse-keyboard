The Model 3 Standard Range has a range of approximately 263 miles (423 km) and offers a top speed of 140 mph (225 km/h). It can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 97 km/h) in just 5.3 seconds.

What is the battery capacity of the Model 3 Standard Range?

The Model 3 Standard Range has a battery capacity of 54 kWh.

What are the charging options available for the Model 3 Standard Range?

The Model 3 Standard Range can be charged at home using a standard electrical outlet or through a dedicated Tesla Wall Connector. It is also compatible with Tesla Superchargers, allowing for faster charging.

The Model 3 Standard Range boasts a sleek and modern design, with smooth lines and a streamlined profile. It features Tesla's signature front grille-less fascia and aerodynamic wheels.
